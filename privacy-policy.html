Privacy Policy

Bead Pop · Last updated: July 20, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Bead Pop ("the App", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our mobile game. By using Bead Pop, you agree to the practices described below.

In short: Bead Pop is a puzzle game. You can play without giving us any personal identifiers. You may optionally link a Google or Apple account to sync progress across devices, which shares your email with us. We use anonymous accounts to save your progress in the cloud, a payments provider to handle optional purchases, and an advertising provider to show ads (which you can remove with a Premium purchase). We do not sell your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information you provide

Bead Pop does not require you to create an account to play. Optionally, you may link a Google or Apple account (Settings → Account) to save your progress and restore it on another device. If you choose to link an account, we receive the email address associated with that provider, used solely to identify and sync your saved game data. Linking is entirely optional and you can play the full game without it. We do not collect your phone number or physical address.

1.2 Game data (cloud sync)

To save and synchronize your progress across sessions and devices, the App creates an anonymous account through our backend provider (Supabase). This account is not linked to your real-world identity. The following gameplay data is stored:

  • Level unlock status and best completion times
  • In-game virtual currency balances (coins and hints)
  • Daily challenge streak and last-completed date
  • Premium entitlement status
  • Preferences such as whether sound is enabled

1.3 Purchase data

When you make an optional in-app purchase (hint packs, coin packs, or Premium subscriptions), the transaction is processed by the Apple App Store or Google Play Store and by our purchase-management provider, RevenueCat. We do not receive or store your credit card number or full payment details. We receive a purchase receipt and an anonymous purchaser identifier used to grant your entitlements.

1.4 Advertising data

Bead Pop displays ads through Google AdMob. To serve ads, AdMob and its partners may collect device-level information such as a device advertising identifier (IDFA / Android Advertising ID), IP address, device type, and general usage/interaction data. Purchasing Premium removes all ads, after which no further ad requests are made.

1.5 Local data on your device

Some settings and cached progress are stored locally on your device (via on-device storage and local notifications). This data stays on your device unless synced as described above.

2. How We Use Information

  • To save your game progress and let you continue where you left off
  • To synchronize progress across your devices
  • To process and restore optional purchases and grant Premium benefits
  • To send local reminder notifications for the daily challenge (only if you allow notifications)
  • To display and measure advertising (unless you have Premium)
  • To maintain, secure, and improve the App

4. Advertising Choices

You can limit ad personalization at any time through your device settings:

  • iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking, and Settings → Privacy & Security → Apple Advertising.
  • Android: Settings → Privacy → Ads (reset or delete advertising ID).

Purchasing Premium disables ads entirely.

5. Data Retention

We retain your anonymous game data for as long as your account remains active so your progress is preserved. Because accounts are anonymous, uninstalling the App without a linked identity may make your saved cloud data unrecoverable. You may request deletion of your data as described in Section 8.

6. Data Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect data transmitted to and stored by our providers, including encryption in transit. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Children's Privacy

Bead Pop is a general-audience game and is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or the applicable minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can remove it.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location (for example, under the GDPR or CCPA), you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your data, or to object to certain processing.

You can delete your account and all associated data directly in the app at any time: go to Settings → Account → Delete account. This permanently erases your cloud profile (progress, virtual currency balances, and any linked Google/Apple identity) and cannot be undone. For any other request, contact us at the email below.

9. International Data Transfers

Our providers may process and store data on servers located outside your country of residence. By using the App, you consent to such transfers in accordance with this Policy and the providers' own safeguards.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Your continued use of the App after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the revised Policy.
