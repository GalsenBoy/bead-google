Delete Your Account

Bead Pop · Last updated: July 20, 2026

This page explains how to delete your Bead Pop account and the data associated with it. You can do this yourself, directly inside the app, at any time.

Delete your account in the app

  1. Open Bead Pop on your device.
  2. Go to the Settings tab (the gear icon in the bottom bar).
  3. Scroll to the Account section.
  4. Tap Delete account.
  5. Confirm by tapping Delete in the dialog that appears.

Your account and its data are removed immediately. The app then starts fresh as a new guest.

This is permanent. Deleting your account cannot be undone. Your progress, virtual currency, and any linked sign-in cannot be recovered afterward.

What gets deleted

When you delete your account, we permanently erase:

  • Your cloud profile and game progress (level unlocks and best times)
  • Your in-game virtual currency balances (coins and hints)
  • Your daily challenge streak and history
  • Any linked Google or Apple sign-in identity
  • Your locally stored game data on the device

What this does not affect

  • Purchases and subscriptions. In-app purchases are managed by the Apple App Store or Google Play, not by Bead Pop. To cancel or request a refund for a subscription, use your store account's subscription settings. Deleting your Bead Pop account does not cancel a store subscription.
  • Store account. Your Apple or Google account itself is not affected — only the link between it and Bead Pop is removed.
